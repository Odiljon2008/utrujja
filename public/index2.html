About this role

We are seeking a skilled Front-End Developer to create a comprehensive web application for our AI-based hiring platform. The ideal candidate will craft an engaging user experience with exceptional UI/UX design, ensuring simplicity and functionality across various sections including a home page, login panel, client dashboard, AI-driven engineer recommendation system, and a tracking panel for client hires.

Responsibilities

-> Developing the front-end elements of a web application that aligns with our AI-based hiring platform's vision.

-> Implementing a user-friendly home page that effectively showcases our offerings.

-> Creating a secure login panel that leads to a feature-rich client dashboard.

-> Integrating an AI-powered recommendation engine to assist clients in finding suitable candidates.

-> Building a tracking panel for clients to monitor the hours worked by their hires.

-> Ensuring the entire web application is bug-free and easy to navigate for clients.
